About the event

Music... Masti... Fun!! Pack your bags for zippy weekend this 29th and 30th of March, as Prayag'19, the annual cultural fest of Defence Institute of Advanced Technology (DU) (Funded by DRDO) unveils its glory to students and participants from colleges all around in the country this year, for the first time. Be the part of the most happening event in the city of Pune to witness ecstatic performances by participants in various competitions and a music night with a famous band and DJs.

Block and Tackle

Block and Tackle is a kind of debate discussion or structured contest about an issue or a resolution. This game involves two sides: one supporting a resolution and one opposing it. Debate is bound by rules previously agreed upon.

Rules & Regulations
  1. This event will be organized between two teams with three participants in each team.
  2. The topics will be given On spot.
  3. One team will be against the topic and vice versa.
  4. The winner team will be decided based on clarity of topic, how you deliver, presentation of your point and other factors decided by the judges present there.

Tambola

Rules & Regulations
  1. Every participant should buy a ticket which contains 15 numbers from 1 to 90.
  2. The host will randomly pick a number(1-90) from a bowl of numbers and read it out to the audience and place it on a number board.
  3. The participants must mark the numbers which are announced on their tickets.
  4. Those who finish 1st /2nd/3rd row or full ticket before others will be awarded with cash prize (more number of prizes will be added depending on the number of participants).
  5. Spot registration.

Ad mad show

Rules & Regulations

  2. The team will pick up a product randomly from a bowl of chits.
  3. The team will be given a time period of 10 mins after which they must advertise the product – make a skit / song / dance / poetry or likewise.

Recitation

  4. 01 Hard copy of the poem to be submitted to event organizer at the time of the event.
  5. They are allowed to read from the text but bonus marks for memorization.
  6. A sample for shortlisting purpose to recitation.prayagfest@gmail.com.
  7. Judges’ decision will be final.
  8. Spot registrations.
